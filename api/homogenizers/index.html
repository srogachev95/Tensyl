ValidityContext dataclass

Optional geometric scale data for tangent-plane validity checks.

characteristic_height is a stiffness or stiffener height scale, pitch is the repeated-cell spacing, min_radius is the smallest local curvature radius, and response_length is the intended structural response length such as a buckle wavelength or analysis feature size.

Direct equilibrium-compatibility homogenizer for straight member families.
